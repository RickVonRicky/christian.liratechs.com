ABOUT THE ARTIST

Betty White is a street artist whose signature character — Kip the Frog — started as a stencil on a forgotten wall and became a movement. Part protest, part poetry, every piece tells a story the city doesn't want you to hear.

Working across murals, canvas, and limited-edition prints, Betty White blends raw urban energy with sharp social commentary. Kip shows up where you least expect him — spray can in hand, truth on the wall.
